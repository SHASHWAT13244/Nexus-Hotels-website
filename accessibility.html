Accessibility Statement

Committed to providing an inclusive experience for all guests

Our Commitment

Nexus Hotels is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following features:

  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Screen reader compatibility
  • Alternative text for images
  • Resizable text without loss of functionality
  • High contrast mode
  • Clear and consistent navigation

Hotel Accessibility

Our physical locations offer:

  • Accessible guest rooms with roll-in showers
  • Wheelchair-accessible public spaces
  • Visual alarms and notification devices
  • TTY/TTD compatible phones
  • Service animal accommodations
  • Trained staff to assist guests with disabilities

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website and properties. Please let us know if you encounter any barriers:

  • Email: accessibility@nexushotels.com
  • Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567 (Voice/TTY)
  • Mail: 123 Future Avenue, Tech District, NY 10001

Standards Compliance

We aim to meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards for web accessibility. While we strive to adhere to these guidelines, some third-party content may not be fully accessible.

Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is an ongoing effort at Nexus Hotels. We regularly review our website and properties to identify and address accessibility issues.
