Sustainability Report

Our commitment to a greener future

Our Sustainability Vision

At Nexus Hotels, we believe luxury and sustainability can coexist. Our vision is to create exceptional guest experiences while minimizing our environmental impact and contributing positively to our communities.

Environmental Initiatives

Renewable Energy

100% of our electricity comes from renewable sources, including on-site solar panels and wind power purchases.

Waste Reduction

We've achieved 85% waste diversion from landfills through comprehensive recycling and composting programs.

Green Building

All properties are LEED Gold certified, featuring energy-efficient systems and sustainable materials.

Water Conservation

Low-flow fixtures and water recycling systems have reduced our water usage by 40% compared to industry standards.

2025 Sustainability Goals

85%

Carbon Neutral Operations

On track to achieve carbon neutrality by 2026 through emission reductions and verified offsets.

90%

Zero Single-Use Plastics

Eliminated 90% of single-use plastics from our operations with a plan to reach 100% by Q3 2025.

75%

Local Sourcing

75% of our food and beverages are now sourced within 100 miles, supporting local farmers and reducing transport emissions.

Community Engagement

We partner with local organizations to support environmental education, urban green spaces, and sustainable tourism initiatives. In 2024, our employees volunteered over 5,000 hours in community projects.

Certifications & Awards

  • LEED Gold Certification for all properties
  • Green Key Global 5-Key Rating
  • 2024 Sustainable Hospitality Award
  • Carbon Neutral Certified (Scope 1 & 2)
